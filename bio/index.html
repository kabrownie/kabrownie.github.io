Kevin Karanja Bio

Born and raised in the vibrant city of Nakuru, Kevin Karanja's journey took a significant turn when his family relocated to Nairobi in 2009. It was in Nairobi where Kevin's academic and extracurricular pursuits began to shape his future. Kevin's educational journey commenced at Mwiki Primary School, where he laid the foundation for his academic excellence. Graduating with an impressive 351 marks, he showcased early signs of determination and commitment to his studies.

Transitioning to Weithaga Boys High School (muran'ga), Kevin continued to excel academically, achieving a remarkable 69 points in his Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education (KCSE) examinations, earning him a commendable B+ grade. This achievement underscored his dedication to academic excellence and paved the way for his higher education pursuits.

With a passion for technology ignited, Kevin enrolled at Dedan Kimathi University of Technology (DeKUT) to pursue a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science. It was during his time at DeKUT that Kevin's journey took an exciting turn as he delved deeper into the world of technology and computer science.

Eager to expand his horizons beyond academics, Kevin joined the university's chess club, where he honed his strategic thinking and analytical skills. His dedication and enthusiasm for the game led to him becoming a chess tutor. Teaching primary and highschool students how to play chess all thanks to the quo vadis youths hub.

Kevin's involvement in the chess community extended beyond the university campus, as he actively participated in various chess tournaments, showcasing his prowess on the board and earning accolades for his strategic prowess.
