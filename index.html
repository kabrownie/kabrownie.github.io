Who is this?

I’m Kevin Karanja, a 3D animator, 3D artist, and software developer from Kenya. I gained an interest in animation after completing the Computer Graphics unit in my computer science degree. That curiosity eventually led me to earn a Certificate in Animation and Visual Effects. I love creating stories with Blender, whether it’s modelling, sculpting, rigging, or full 3D animation. On the tech side, I develop Android apps with Java/Kotlin and web projects with Laravel and Django. When I’m not coding or animating, I enjoy making original music with FL Studio and playing chess.
